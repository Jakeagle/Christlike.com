About us

We are a discord server dedicated to helping those who believe in Christianity gain fellowship, grow their faith, and spread the gospel of Jesus Christ. Were a very tightly knit community who'd be more than happy to welcome you in!

So if youre interested in what we have to offer, read below, and be sure to join us wed be more than happy to have you with us!
